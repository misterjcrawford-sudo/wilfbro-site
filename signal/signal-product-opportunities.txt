SIGNAL — PRODUCT & OPPORTUNITY ROADMAP
=======================================


THE CORE ASSET
──────────────
SIGNAL's value is not templates or scripts — it's a coherent intellectual
framework. Everything below is a delivery mechanism for that framework.
The question worth asking for each: what's the highest-value delivery
mechanism for the person you're building for?

IMMEDIATE EXTENSIONS — Low lift, high value
────────────────────────────────────────────

Appraisal Prep Brief Generator
  Agent fills in 5–6 fields about an upcoming seller (situation,
  how they found the agent, property details) and the tool outputs
  a one-page brief: likely archetype, dominant fear, what to lead
  with, what to avoid, three suggested questions to ask. Replaces
  the prompt pack workflow with something faster and more guided.

Claim Builder Tool
  Agent inputs their raw data (sales count, days on market, referral
  rate, suburb tenure) and the tool writes their four claim types in
  polished, verifiable language. The draft work for the claim
  architecture prompt pack — but without needing to open an AI
  separately. Interactive, instant, exportable.

MEDIUM-TERM TOOLS — More build, high value
───────────────────────────────────────────

Pipeline Tracker
  A simple spreadsheet or lightweight app where agents tag each warm
  contact with their decision stage (1–5) and suspected archetype.
  Surfaces: who to contact this week, which conditioning actions are
  overdue, concentration gaps. The 30-Day Sprint as a living dashboard
  rather than a static document.

Seller Archetype Quiz
  A short diagnostic agents send to sellers before the appraisal, or
  work through with them at the start of a meeting. "Tell me about
  your situation" framed as a questionnaire. Outputs the archetype
  profile as a shared document — a surprisingly powerful trust move
  that differentiates the agent before a word is spoken.

Agent Archetype Self-Assessment
  The same quiz logic turned inward. Agents answer ~15 questions about
  how they naturally behave in meetings and get their archetype result
  with a breakdown of their natural match sellers and friction zones.
  Entry point into the full system for new users.

BIGGER PLAYS — Larger opportunity
───────────────────────────────────

Team / Agency Version
  A principal buys access for their whole team. Each agent has their
  archetype profile. The tool maps the team's collective coverage:
  which seller types are well-covered, where gaps exist, who should
  take which listing. SIGNAL as an agency management and team
  development tool, not just an individual one.

The SIGNAL Card Set
  The 6 seller archetypes as a physical pocket-sized reference deck
  agents carry to appraisals. High perceived value, giftable,
  memorable. A physical product with strong gifting potential for
  principals and coaches.

Training Program / Workshop
  The three layers as a structured workshop. Sold to agencies as a
  half-day or full-day session. Content is already structured for
  delivery — each layer is essentially a module. Scalable via
  accredited trainers or agency partnerships.

Market Expansion
  The framework is currently Australian-specific but the psychology
  is universal. A UK or US adaptation requires mostly surface-level
  changes — terminology, market references, currency. Different
  distribution but the same core IP.

THE QUESTION TO KEEP ASKING
────────────────────────────
Which delivery mechanism creates the most value for the person using it?

  → Solo agent, 10 minutes before an appraisal
     = Appraisal Prep Brief / Archetype Quiz

  → Agent building their market presence over 30 days
     = Pipeline Tracker / Sprint Dashboard

  → Agency principal investing in team performance
     = Team Version / Workshop

  → Someone discovering SIGNAL for the first time
     = Agent Archetype Self-Assessment (entry point)
