What objection are you facing?

Pick the one in front of you. Get the real issue, your approach, and the exact line to use.

01
"Your fee is too high"
They're comparing your number to a cheaper alternative.
02
"Another agent quoted us more"
A competitor's price guide is higher than yours.
03
"We want to try it privately first"
They want to sell without an agent to save on fees.
04
"We'll wait for the market"
They want to time the market before listing.
05
"We're still deciding between agents"
You're in the final shortlist but haven't won it yet.
06
"Your price guide is too low"
They expect more than you can support with data.
