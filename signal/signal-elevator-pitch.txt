SIGNAL — ELEVATOR PITCH
=======================


ONE-LINERS
──────────

Most agents prepare for the appraisal. SIGNAL prepares the seller.

SIGNAL is a seller psychology system that helps listing agents win
the appraisal before they walk in the door.

SIGNAL maps how sellers actually decide — and gives agents the
framework to influence that decision before it's made.


30-SECOND SPOKEN PITCH
───────────────────────

"Most agents think the listing is won in the room — at the appraisal.
SIGNAL is built on a different idea: by the time a seller calls you
in, they've already formed a view. SIGNAL is a system that helps you
understand how your specific seller thinks, what they fear, what they
need to hear — and how to position yourself as the obvious choice
before you ever sit down with them. It's psychology-led, it's
practical, and it works whether you're a seasoned agent or just
starting out."
