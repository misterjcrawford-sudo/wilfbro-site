/ Layer 01 — The Seller Decision Window

The seller decides across 5 stages.
Most agents only show up at stage 4.

/ Stage 01

Private Consideration

They haven't told anyone. Quietly wondering if now is the right time. No agent knows this is happening.

Signal: Physical presence & familiarity

/ Stage 02

Market Curiosity

Attending opens. Reading suburb reports. Forming a view of the market — and of who the credible agents are.

Signal: Authority & local data

/ Stage 03

Agent Awareness

Noticing who has signs up, who sends good content, who their neighbours used. A shortlist is forming unconsciously.

Signal: Consistency & social proof

/ Stage 04

Appraisal Mode

Calling 2–3 agents. The decision is mostly made. This is confirmation, not comparison. Most agents think this is where they win.

Signal: Rehearsed posture & proof

/ Stage 05

Decision Crystallisation

Choosing. Often on gut feel that was formed weeks ago. The agent who conditioned stages 1–3 wins this almost automatically.

Signal: You've already won
